# What Do I Do

## Register
product

## Platform
web

## Target Users
Single user, local-first, personal life management. One device, one person. Power user who tracks daily time, tasks, habits, goals, and money across a structured workflow.

## Product Purpose
Replace scattered habit trackers, planners, and budgeting apps with one integrated local system. No cloud, no sync, no multi-user — just fast, private, personal orchestration.

## Brand Personality
Empathetic but structured. Feels like a kind taskmaster: warm, encouraging, but never fuzzy. Claymorphism visual language (rounded heavy surfaces, soft shadows, tactile) communicates "solid but gentle" — it's a tool you trust with your daily data.

## Anti-references
- Not Notion (no blank canvas anxiety)
- No gamification-for-its-own sake (achievements are earned patterns, not badges for everything)
- No SaaS-chatty onboarding ("let's get started!" wizard)
- No dark patterns, no upsells

## Strategic Principles
1. **Local-first, no excuses**: zero network dependency for any core flow
2. **Recovery over perfection**: timer survives app-kill, offline buffer for everything
3. **Insight before raw data**: analytics and insights are first-class citizens, not afterthoughts
4. **Claymorphism tactile warmth**: heavy surfaces, soft shadows, rounded — data apps should feel solid, not clinical

## Offline/Data
Local SQLite via Drizzle. Manual JSON export/import for portability. No sync engine.

## Current Modules
Dashboard, Inbox, Planner, Activity Tracker, Tasks, Habits, Goals, Money, Life Log, Analytics, Statistics, Achievements, Insights, Workspace, Settings.
